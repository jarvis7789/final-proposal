Application

The goal of this is to be able for someone to enter a distance that they are racing, like a 5k or 2 miles, and the goal time they watching to run, and have it spit out the pace you need to go at for different distances like pace per mile or pace per 400 meters. The application would also work the other way, where you input a pace, and it will spit out a time. In longer races, the hundreths or seconds matter a lot so I will make sure that they get accounted for.
